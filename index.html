Our Services

Bedrooms

  • All furniture dusted
  • Mirrors cleaned
  • Window sills cleaned
  • Internal window cleaning
  • Light switches cleaned
  • Carpets vacuumed, including under the bed if accessible
  • Non-carpeted floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Beds made

Bathrooms

  • Bath cleaned, rinsed, and left shining
  • Shower recess scrubbed, fittings and glass cleaned
  • Internal window cleaning
  • Pipe work/radiator washed
  • Basin and vanity scrubbed
  • Toilets scrubbed and disinfected, including base and behind
  • Mirrors cleaned and polished

Kitchen

  • Appliance exteriors cleaned (stovetop, oven, fridge, microwave, dishwasher)
  • Small appliances wiped (toaster, coffee makers)
  • Refrigerator cleaned inside, all grime removed
  • Exterior of refrigerator wiped
  • Standard oven clean (deep cleaning optional)

Stairs, Hallways, and Common Areas

  • All furniture and picture frames dusted
  • All surfaces dusted
  • Light and socket fittings wiped clean
  • Skirting boards damp wiped
  • All cobwebs removed from ceilings/walls
  • Upholstered furniture vacuumed
